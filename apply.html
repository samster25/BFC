Application Process

Thank you for your interest in joining Berkeley Finance Club. We recruit at the beginning of each spring and fall semester, and are open to students with all majors, backgrounds, and grades. We will be tabling on Sproul Plaza, so feel free to stop by and have a chat with us and put your email on the mailing list. We will be holding our information session on February 11th (Tuesday), where details will be announced through the mailing list and on our website. We will also be holding a social night where you will get to know the members a little bit more before you start interviewing with us.


Application Requirement
