Our Members

Berkeley Finance Club is made up of highly-motivated student leaders from a broad range of academic disciplines. Many members juggle a busy schedule between BFC and a variety of extracurricular activities including entrepreneurship, fraternities and sororities, and other business clubs. Outside of their interest in finance, we have members that are real-estate entrepreneur, competitive horse-back rider and table tennis player. Before their junior-summer internships, many members have gained professional experiences through working at private equities, hedge funds, boutique investment banks, trading shops, and investment management firms.

Our Mission

Our core mission is to help undergraduates who are interested in finance reach their individual potential. In a close-knit community, we provide mentorships, professional development, and career networking opportunities. Club members will learn practical knowledge about the financial market and how to land a job in the financial industry. MBA and MFE students, professionals, and other upperclassmen will help our members prepare through resume critique, networking, and interviews.
